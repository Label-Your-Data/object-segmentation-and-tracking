# Nexvision Case Study: Pupil Segmentation and Maritime Object Tracking

Nexvision needed detailed annotations across multiple computer vision tasks to support model training for gaze detection and maritime tracking.

Label Your Data supported the project with high-precision image and video annotation across three parallel workflows:

- 40,000 pupil images annotated with ellipse masks
- 30,000 eye images annotated with bounding boxes
- 4,000 maritime videos annotated with object tracks
- Around 10 annotators involved across all streams
- Rolling batches delivered over approximately 6 months

## Client

Nexvision is a French embedded vision company serving defense, aerospace, security, and industrial sectors. The company builds advanced vision systems and relies on high-quality annotated datasets to train and improve computer vision models.

## Challenges

The project required accurate labels across several different data types and annotation workflows.

**Key challenges included:**

- Pupil detection required ellipse-level precision that automated tools could not reliably deliver
- Maritime footage required manual object tracking with dynamic camera motion
- Tasks were split across different pipelines, including Nexvision’s internal tool and CVAT
- Batch sizes and timelines changed frequently, requiring flexible team scaling and delivery

## Solution

Label Your Data split the work into three parallel annotation workflows. Batches were delivered regularly, with client feedback incorporated between cycles.

### Pupil Segmentation

For pupil segmentation, 40,000 images were labeled using Nexvision’s internal annotation tool. Annotators created precise ellipse masks to support gaze detection model training.

### Eye Detection

For eye detection, 30,000 images were annotated with bounding boxes using CVAT.

### Maritime Object Tracking

For maritime tracking, 4,000 videos were manually labeled with object tracks in CVAT. This helped support model training for maritime awareness and object tracking in video data.

## Training and QA

Each annotation stream started with written guidelines and a kickoff call. Annotators completed small pilot batches before moving to full production. This helped align the team with the client’s expectations early and reduced the need for major rework.

The project included QA and feedback cycles throughout delivery.

## Results

Label Your Data delivered all datasets on time and supported Nexvision’s model training needs across both gaze detection and maritime tracking.

**Key results:**

- 3 complex annotation pipelines delivered in parallel
- 40,000 accurate ellipse masks created for gaze model training
- 30,000 eye images annotated with bounding boxes
- 4,000 maritime videos manually tracked
- Flexible team size adapted to shifting batch scope
- All batches delivered on time
- No quality issues were reported
